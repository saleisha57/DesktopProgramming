Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp Butter
  • 2 Tbsp Flour
  • 1 1/2 Cups Heavy Whipping Cream
  • 8oz Pepperjack Cheese (Shredded)
  • 4oz Medium Cheddar Cheese (Shredded)
  • 2-3 Jalapenos (Finely Diced)
  • 1/4 Cup Diced Green Onion (Optional, I do not use in mine)
  • 3 Garlic Cloves (Minced)
  • 1 Large Red Bell Pepper (Finely Diced)
  • Pinch of Cayenne
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Pre-dice the jalapenos and bell pepper. Set aside.
  2. In a medium sauce pot melt butter.
  3. Add jalapenos, bell peppers, and garlic to the melted butter. Cook until slightly softened.
  4. Sprinkle flour and mix.
  5. Add in the heavy cream and heat.
  6. Add in the cheese ne handul at a time until melted.
  7. Stir in ceyenne and salt.
  8. Serve right away.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Flour
  • 1 Cup Milk
  • 1 Tbsp Oil
  • 2 Eggs
  • Peanut Butter to taste
  • Maple Syrup to taste

Instructions

  1. Place a non-stick large skillet over medium-low heat.
  2. Mix all ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Once the pan is heated pour about 1/4 - 1/3 cup of mixture and immediately tilt the pan until the mixture forms a circular shape. (Should look thin like a crepe)
  4. Cook until the edges just slightly raise up and flip with a spatula.
  5. Cook for another 20 to 30 seconds, then transfer to a plate.
  6. While it's still hot spread peanut butter and pour syrup.
  7. Using a fork pierce the inside of the pancake and roll until completely rolled.

Ingredients

  • Four Tilapia Fillets
  • Plain or Italian Bread Crumbs
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Garlic Powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Grated Parmesan
  • Mayo

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to -temp-, and line baking dish with tin foil and spray with cooking spray.
  2. Wash Tilapia fillets and pat dry with a paper towel. Set aside.
  3. In a medium size mixing bowl combine bread crumbs, italian seasoning, garlic powder, salt, pepper, and grated parmesan.
  4. Using a brush spready mayo onto the Tilapia fillets until fully covered.
  5. One fillet at a time place in the breadbrumb mixture and goat entirely.
  6. Place the fillets on the baking dish and lightly spray with cooking spray. (Do not do too much)
  7. Place the baking dish in the oven and bake for 15 to 20 minutes.
